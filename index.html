Sisyphus Ball 2.0

Backstory

Press ESC at any time to reset the timer/score and go back to the main menu.

You were just a simple basketball... until you bounced off the rim at the buzzer to lose the game winning shot game 7 of the NBA finals. Now you are doomed to make buckets for eternity.

Now your only goal is to score more baskets than the basketball devil, but he has infinite points... what are you going to do?
